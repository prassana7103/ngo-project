How Can You Lend A Helping Hand For Our Furry Friends?

Do you fill sorry for the stray animals when they roam here and there in search of food and shelter? Surely you guys must be feeling to come to the aid of this cute animals and you are at the right place because this site will surely help you to contribute for this strays. Steps are very easy to follow up, firstly if you notice any stray animals wandering around in your locality or if any random location the only thing you guys need to do is to take a photo of that animal, description about the current condition of that stray with the exact location. Now here comes the main process in which we collect all that information from you helpers and forward it to all the NGO's who are registered to our site. First to response to the uploaded data will take responsibility of that dog/cat.
